# 🚀 Life Discipline AI

**Life Discipline AI** is a premium AI-powered discipline, productivity, and accountability platform designed to help users build consistency, optimize routines, improve focus, and achieve long-term goals.

Built with a sleek, high-contrast modern interface, the application combines intelligent coaching, performance analytics, habit tracking, and daily execution monitoring into a unified productivity ecosystem.

---

## ✨ Features

### 🎯 Daily Discipline Score

Track your overall daily performance through a dynamic discipline scoring system.

The score is calculated from:

* Sleep consistency
* Routine completion
* Study sessions
* Workout completion
* Hydration tracking
* Goal progress
* Daily productivity metrics

A real-time circular progress indicator provides instant visual feedback on your daily execution quality.

---

### 📋 Smart Routine Management

Organize and execute daily routines efficiently.

Features include:

* Task categorization
* Priority-based planning
* Adaptive task cards
* One-tap completion tracking
* Custom task creation
* Progress recalculation in real time

Supported Categories:

* Routine
* Study
* Workout
* Work
* Personal Development
* Custom Categories

Priority Levels:

* High
* Medium
* Low

---

### 📊 Analytics & Performance Tracking

Gain deeper insights into your discipline journey.

Track:

* Daily consistency
* Routine completion rates
* Performance trends
* Long-term discipline growth
* Historical progress data

Interactive analytics help users identify strengths, weaknesses, and improvement opportunities.

---

### 🏆 Achievement System

Stay motivated with unlockable achievement badges.

Examples:

* 7 Day Consistency
* 30 Day Discipline Master
* Hydration Hero
* Focus Champion
* Early Riser
* Goal Crusher

Achievements reward long-term commitment and encourage sustainable discipline habits.

### 🤖 Sensei AI Coach

Meet your personal accountability coach.

Sensei AI provides:

* Daily feedback
* Productivity recommendations
* Habit optimization guidance
* Motivation and accountability
* Personalized improvement suggestions

Users can interact naturally through a conversational AI interface.

Example interactions:

* Log wake-up times
* Report completed habits
* Ask productivity questions
* Receive customized coaching

### 🔔 Accountability Notification System

Built-in notification engine for:

* Habit reminders
